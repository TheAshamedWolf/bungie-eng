Above and Beyond

August 2, 1955 served as the starting signal for the greatest scientific competition in the history of humanity.
The Space Race.

In the 14 years that followed, hundreds of thousands of people came togehter, they spent a huge amount of money, made use of a colossal amount of brain power. And they achieved something that was considered impossible in any expectation or prediction.

On July 20, 1969, the first people landed on the moon, marking the end of the race. And although there have been a number of people on the moon until 1972. For the nearly 50 years that followed, nothing or anyone has ever been on the moon again
Until now...
